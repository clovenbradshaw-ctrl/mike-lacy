I’m also rebranding a bit, and I’m happy to tell you a bit about it. I’m going to start writing under the banner “Rich Text”.

There's a branch of philosophy called "deconstructionism” that questions our assumptions about certainty, identity, and truth. In deconstructionism, there’s a term for writing that contains multiple layers of meaning and complexity called a "rich text".

I love to find pieces of “rich text” and unpack their layered meaning to help us understand the world better. A rich text reveals a much broader reality, unfolding new interpretations and perspectives as you explore it.

That’s the kind of thing I love, finding a snippet that doesn’t just teach us something specific, but unlocks our greater understanding in a way that we can take with us and redesign the world around us with that knowledge.
